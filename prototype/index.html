Welcome to 방탄소년단

Who are they?

방탄소년단(Bangtan Sonyeondan) aka BTS, is a south korean boy band whove become a worldwide phenomenom. The band consists of seven members and have been active since June 13, 2013. Since the start of thier musical career, BTS have been smashing records left and right. Even when they struggled at a bit at first, all of their hard work ended up paying off in the end. Now they are loved by many across the world.

"Those who don't have a dream, it's okay, it's okay if you don't have a dream. You just have to be happy. - Min Yoongi

Accomplishments

If you were to make a list of all the awards BTS have won that list would reach all the way to the floor. Some of these awards include: MAMA Artist of the Year, Golden Disc Award Song Division, Billboard Music Award for Top Social Artist, etc. They are even featured in the Guiness World Records with a total of 28 records.

Not only have they created many hits that have toped multiple music charts, they have also been appointed as dimplomatic envoys by the south korean president. Taking on the role of "special presidential envoy for future generation and culture. Having appeared 2 times at the UN General Assembly so far. Before that, they had already started the Love Myself campaign in partnership with UNICEF in order to help end violence against children and young people.

Fan Base

People who are fans of BTS call themselves ARMY aka Adorable Representative M.C. For Youth. Just like BTS themselves, many ARMYs from around the world help organize fundraisers in order to support orginizations such as the Love Myself campaign. Due to ARMY being very orginazed when it comes to helping break records, its of no surprise when they come together in order to support a good cause. ARMY even create donation projects in honor of each of the members birthdays. Making donations for the Hungry International Federation, SASCO Senior Citizen's Home for Singpore, Plant a Tree, and many more.
